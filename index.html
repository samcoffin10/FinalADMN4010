About Us

Company History

Sam's Surf Supply was founded in July of 2020 with the goal of sustainably cleaning waste out of ocean and recycling that material into clothing. We started in a small town in New Hampshire, Portsmouth. From there we started out making hats from recycled plastics cleaned out of the ocean, and in the present day just released a new line of Wetsuits.

Why Choose Us

We are a local surf apparrel company and are dedicated to sustainability and making a positive impact on the world. Our business practices listed below help us hold true to that claim:

  • Top of the line manufacturing facilities to ensure quality construction
  • High-quality products made from recycled materials
  • 0 harmful emissions into the atmosphere during productuon
